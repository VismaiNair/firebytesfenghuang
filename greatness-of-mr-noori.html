The Greatness of Mr. Noori

Who is Mr. Noori?

Mr. Noori is an English teacher at BASIS Phoenix. He teaches classes in greades 6 and 7, and is one of the club advisors for Firebytes Robotics. He is the teacher for Firebytes Feng Huang, and has done much to support the team, including:

  • Learning and teaching us how to code the robot
  • Spending hours researching the challenges so that he can guide us
  • Informing us of the Innovation Project and guiding discussions about the Innovation Project
  • Being a great inspiration to everyone on Firebytes Feng Huang

Mr. Noori is a great inspiration to everyone, and we appreciate his hard work to ensure that Firebytes Feng Huang still has a chance at the competition. It is only because of Mr. Noori that Firebytes Feng Huang RISEs and works together to try and win.
