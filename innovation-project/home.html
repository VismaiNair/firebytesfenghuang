The Innovation Project




THE PROBLEM

Written literature is an artistic thing in its own right, yet it is not treated as such. Descriptive writing can paint a picture in people's minds just as well as music and art, yet it is not recognized, especially in the form of wordplay, riddles, and poetry.

RESEARCH

We're collecting research! It's not too late to contribute.

Research collection will end on December 10, 2023, and research will be published the same day.

OUR SOLUTIONS

Poems

We believe that poems are one of the most artistic forms of writing, yet they are not appreciated because it is hard to find a collection of decent poems. We've built a generator to randomly pick a poem from a curated database and show it to you.

Riddles

Because riddles are not easily accessible, we've built a short game to introduce people to the beauty of riddles. Ready to Play?
